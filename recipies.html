chicken biryani

ingredients

  • main ingredients
    1. checking-500gm
    2. basmati rice 2cups
    3. Onion – 3 large (thinly sliced)
    4. tomato – 2 medium (optional, chopped)
    5. cilies – 2–3 (slit)
    6. Curd (yogurt) – 1 cup
    7. Fresh coriander leaves – ½ cup
    8. Fresh mint leaves – ½ cup
  • Spices & Masalas
    1. Red chili powder – 1½ tsp
    2. Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
    3. cnder powder – 1½ tsp
    4. m masala – 1 tsp
    5. bni masala – 1–2 tsp

instructions

    Don’t overcook rice (will become mushy)
    Don’t use too much water for rice
    Don’t skip marination
    Don’t cook chicken fully before dum
    Don’t add too much garam masala
    Don’t stir biryani during dum cooking
    Don’t open lid frequently while dum
    Don’t use high flame for dum
    Don’t add raw rice directly to masala
    Don’t rush the process
